Our Philosophy

Restoration is not a luxury.
It is the destination.

At the heart of our philosophy lies a simple belief: true hospitality is not only about where you stay, but how you feel while you are there. We believe in creating a place where comfort, wellbeing, and meaningful experiences come together in perfect balance.

Rooted in the natural beauty and restorative spirit of our surroundings, our hotel is designed as a sanctuary of calm — a place where guests are invited to slow down, reconnect, and experience a more conscious way of living. From the soothing presence of thermal water to the quiet elegance of our spaces, every detail is thoughtfully curated to inspire rest, renewal, and a sense of effortless wellbeing.

More than a destination, we offer an experience shaped by warmth, authenticity, and refined simplicity — where every stay becomes an invitation to restore, rebalance, and truly unwind.

Artermision Spa

Thermal Spa
Treatments

Discover a restorative wellness experience designed to support recovery, rejuvenation, and long-term wellbeing. Our carefully curated thermal spa treatments combine the healing benefits of natural thermal water with personalised wellness guidance to help restore balance, vitality, and inner harmony.

From rejuvenating anti-ageing therapies and aesthetic treatments to tailored nutrition guidance, pool therapy, and specialised treatments for the body and face, each experience is thoughtfully designed to meet your individual needs and support a more holistic approach to health.

Reconnect with your body, restore from within, and experience the therapeutic benefits of treatments designed to promote relaxation, recovery, and overall wellbeing.

Artermision Spa

Therapeutic Massage

A deeply restorative treatment combining skilled therapeutic pressure, relaxation techniques and rhythmic movement. Tailored to each guest's individual needs — whether you seek relief from muscle tension, improved circulation or simply a profound state of calm. Available as a full-body or targeted session.

Artermision Spa

Hydrotherapy

The therapeutic use of water in varied temperatures and pressures to stimulate circulation, ease joint and muscle discomfort and support the body's natural healing response. Our hydrotherapy treatments draw on the unique mineral richness of Edipsos' thermal waters for amplified restorative benefit.

Artermision Spa

Rasul

A traditional Middle Eastern ritual cleansing ceremony. Aromatic mineral muds are applied to the body, followed by a gentle steam session and a refreshing shower. Deeply purifying, intensely relaxing and profoundly restorative for the skin. An experience that cleanses, renews and stills the mind.

Artermision Spa

Salt Therapy

Inspired by the natural salt caves of Eastern Europe, our salt therapy room uses micro-particles of pharmaceutical-grade salt to support respiratory wellness, skin clarity and deep relaxation. A peaceful, breath-focused experience that cleanses the airways and calms the nervous system from within.

Artermision Spa

Head Spa

A deeply restorative scalp and head ritual combining warm oil application, targeted acupressure and gentle massage techniques. Designed to release the tension held in the scalp, neck and shoulders, restore mental clarity and guide you into a state of profound, unhurried calm.

Artermision Spa

Presotherapy

A specialised compression therapy that uses controlled air pressure to stimulate lymphatic drainage, improve circulation and reduce fluid retention. Delivered through a comfortable, full-length garment that gently massages the body in rhythmic waves — leaving you feeling lighter, refreshed and deeply restored.

Artermision Spa

Holistic Facial Treatment

A luxurious facial ritual that treats the skin and the senses in equal measure. Using premium botanical formulations, gentle massage and targeted techniques, this treatment deeply cleanses, hydrates and revitalises the complexion — leaving the skin luminous, balanced and profoundly refreshed. Tailored to your skin type and condition.

The Pool

Thermal Spa Pool

Immerse yourself in the healing waters of Edipsos, naturally enriched with renowned thermal properties and maintained at a soothing temperature of 32–33°C. Designed for deep relaxation and wellbeing, our thermal spa pool offers a restorative experience that combines comfort, serenity and the timeless therapeutic benefits of Edipsos' natural springs.

Benefits: Promotes deep relaxation, supports circulation, relieves muscle tension, eases joint discomfort, reduces stress and enhances overall wellbeing through the natural therapeutic properties of Edipsos thermal water.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who We Are

Artermision Hotel & Spa ("we", "us", "our") is located at Filellinon 5, Loutra Edipsou, 34300, Evia, Greece. We are committed to protecting the personal information of our guests and website visitors in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) and applicable Greek law.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal data: name, email address, phone number, postal address, payment details (processed securely via third-party providers), and stay preferences. We also collect standard website usage data (cookies, IP addresses, browser type) through our analytics tools.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to process reservations, communicate booking confirmations, personalise your stay, respond to enquiries, send newsletters (only with your consent), and meet our legal obligations.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with booking platforms (e.g. Booking.com), payment processors, and service providers strictly necessary for your reservation. All third parties are bound by appropriate data protection agreements.

5. Data Retention

Reservation and guest records are retained for up to 5 years as required by Greek fiscal and hospitality regulations. Marketing data is retained until you withdraw consent.

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR you have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. To exercise your rights, please contact us at info@artermision.gr.

7. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies for functionality and, with your consent, analytics cookies to improve your experience. You may adjust cookie preferences through your browser settings.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact our Data Protection Officer at info@artermision.gr or by post at the address above.

Terms of Service

Last updated: April 2026

1. Reservations

All reservations are subject to availability. A booking is confirmed only upon receipt of a written confirmation from Artermision Hotel & Spa or an authorised booking platform. Guests must be 18 years of age or older to make a reservation.

2. Check-In & Check-Out

Standard check-in time is 14:00 (2 PM) and check-out is 12:00 (noon). Early check-in and late check-out may be arranged subject to availability and may incur an additional charge.

3. Cancellation & Modification Policy

Cancellations made more than 7 days prior to arrival will receive a full refund. Cancellations within 7 days of arrival will be charged one night's accommodation. No-shows will be charged the full reservation amount. Please refer to your booking confirmation for rate-specific terms.

4. Payment

A valid credit or debit card is required at check-in. Payment in full is due upon arrival unless otherwise agreed. We accept Visa, Mastercard, and cash in Euro (€).

5. Hotel Rules

Guests are expected to respect the peace and comfort of fellow guests. Smoking is not permitted inside any hotel building. Pets are not permitted on the premises. The hotel accepts no liability for loss of personal valuables unless stored in the in-room safe or hotel safety deposit box.

6. Spa & Wellness Facilities

Use of spa and wellness facilities is subject to separate booking and health declarations where applicable. Certain treatments may not be suitable for guests with specific medical conditions. Please consult our spa team prior to booking.

7. Limitation of Liability

Artermision Hotel & Spa shall not be liable for any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from your stay, except where required by applicable Greek consumer law.

8. Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of Greece. Any disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Chalcis, Evia, Greece.
