About Us

Where Strength
Meets Balance

At CORE X, we believe in purposeful movement. Our studio combines the precision of Pilates with the power of strength training and the energy of cardio to create a holistic fitness experience that transforms your body and mind.

Located in the heart of Athens, our state-of-the-art facility features premium Pilates reformers, dedicated strength training equipment, and a modern cardio zone — all designed to help you achieve your fitness goals in an inspiring environment.

Our Services

Pilates Reformer

Strength • Control • Balance

Experience the transformative power of reformer Pilates. Our premium equipment and expert instructors guide you through precise movements that strengthen your core, improve flexibility, and enhance body awareness.

Strength Training

Power • Focus • Results

Build functional strength with our comprehensive training programs. From free weights to resistance machines, develop power and definition in a focused, supportive environment.

Cardio Zone

Endurance • Energy • Progress

Boost your cardiovascular fitness with our dedicated cardio zone. State-of-the-art treadmills, bikes, and rowing machines help you build endurance and track your progress.
